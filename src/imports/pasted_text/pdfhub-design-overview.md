PDFHub — Modern All-in-One PDF Toolkit UI/UX Design
Project Overview

Design a premium, modern, clean web application called PDFHub, an all-in-one online PDF toolkit inspired by iLovePDF, Smallpdf, and Adobe Acrobat Online.

The product should feel trustworthy, fast, and effortless to use. Prioritize simplicity, clarity, and accessibility while maintaining a polished SaaS appearance.

The design language should communicate:

Professional
Minimalist
Modern
Friendly
Reliable
Productivity-focused

Avoid visual clutter. Every screen should feel spacious with excellent spacing and hierarchy.
